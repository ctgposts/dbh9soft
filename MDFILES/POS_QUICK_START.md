## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Can I use multiple filters together?**
A: Yes! Use as many filters as you want. They work together.

**Q: What if I forget to clear filters?**
A: Click "✕ Clear All" button in the top right.

**Q: Will my favorites save after I close the browser?**
A: Not yet - they save during your POS session. Coming soon: saving to database.

**Q: Can I sort by popularity?**
A: Yes! Click ⚙️ Filters → Sort By → "Best Seller"

**Q: How do I search for a specific barcode?**
A: Just type it in the search box - it searches barcodes too!

**Q: Can I reuse my search history after closing the app?**
A: Not yet - history is per session. Phase 2 will save it permanently.

**Q: Why is my search slow sometimes?**
A: It shouldn't be! First search takes ~50ms. Report if slower.

**Q: Can I combine size 52 AND 54?**
A: Currently one at a time. Multi-size search coming in Phase 2.
